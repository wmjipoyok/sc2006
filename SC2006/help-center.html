User Login
After the user has registered for an account, they can enter the user credentials and click “Login”. You should be brought to the Map Page if you logged in successfully.


Steps:
1. Key in credentials, and click the blue “Login” button.


2. If successful, you will see this page.


Note: You will be prompted with an error message when:
Username is blank
Password is blank
User ID/Password is incorrect
Forget Password
If you have forgotten your password, you may reset it by clicking “Forgot Password?”. This will direct you to a page to key in your registered email address and click “Reset Password”. An email notification will be sent to you. Check your email and follow the instructions within the sent email. If followed correctly, you will be able to reset your password.


Steps:
1. Click “Forget Password?” and reach the following page, key in your registered email address


2. After keying in your email and there is no error, you would receive the following page. Proceed to your registered email and follow the instructions to reset your password.


User Registration
Firstly, you need to get an account to access the features of the application. After clicking “Create an Account!”, you will be brought to the Create Account page. After filling the credentials, click “Register Account”. If done correctly, you would have successfully registered your account.


Steps:
1. Click “Create an Account!” at the bottom. It will lead to the following page.


2. If credentials are filled correctly, you would receive this page and you would be able to login with your new account.

Note: You will be prompted an error message if: Any of the credentials are empty Password and Repeat Password are different Email address entered is already registered
Rent Car
Within the main page of the Map, you would see many Car Park Icons. Click on the Car Park Icon which you desire to rent from, and click the “View Car List” button. Afterwards, the page would show all available cars within that carpark. Click on the car you desire, and it will bring you to the Car Description Page. Proceed to indicate your Trip Start and Trip End Dates and press the “Book” button. You will have successfully booked your car and can view it within your profile.


Steps:
1. Within the Map Page, click on your desired Car Park (the Car Park Icon) and click “View Car List”


2. Click on your desired Cars which are available within the Car Park.


3. Indicate the time frame which you want to book your cars for. Press the “Book” button after you are done.


Upload for Rent
Within the main page of the Map, you would see many Car Park Icons. Click on the Car Park Icon which you desire to rent your car to, and click the “Add Car” button. Fill in the details of your car, and after you are done click the "Save" button. You will have successfully listed your car and can view it within your profile.


Steps:
1. Within the Map Page, click on your desired Car Park (the Car Park Icon) and click “Add Car”


2. Fill in the details of your car. Afterwards click the blue "Save" button at the bottom.


Note: You will be prompted with an error message when any of the following are blank:
Brand
Model
Seats
Price per day
